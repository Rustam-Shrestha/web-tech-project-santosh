What is Green Camp?

Greencamp is the indigenous bootcamp opened for assisting anyone who is interested in geting into field like Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, NLP and Data Science that provides whole rage of couses in fractional cost of university and helps the learners get into big tech companies like Amazon, Tiktok, Google and X



About this company.

This company was founded by Santosh Shrestha a Climate change activist and environmentalist who hold degree in Data Science and worked in Apple inc for 5 years and has connection with numerous expert in the field all with the same opinion of making the world educated in Futuristic technplogy so no one will be deprived of knowledge that will make them thrive in professional career.
